How It Works

Create an account, upload some songs, create a game, & invite your friends.

Every day a new round starts and a random song is selected (or any link upload). The players guess who submitted it. The first person to guess right wins the round, otherwise the person who submitted the song wins.

Currently guessing happens outside the app in a slack channel or group chat, then a moderator selects the winner. Eventually you'll be able to guess in the app. This is just a side project for fun, so we'll see!

This is how we start our daily standup meetings at my workplace and it keeps things interesting.
